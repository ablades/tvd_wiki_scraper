    BONNIE: And you would've done it knowing that Enzo was the one fighting Sybil. The one risking his life to help us.
    STEFAN: Yes.
    BONNIE: Let me ask you something. What has Damon done? Besides give up.

    [Bonnie walks away, Caroline coming to comfort Stefan.]

    CAROLINE: She doesn't mean that. She's just upset. I'm gonna go stay with Bonnie tonight. She needs me. This has all been a lot for her.
    STEFAN: I hated us being on the opposite side of the fight today.
    CAROLINE: I had to.
    STEFAN: I know. I know what you had to do. But I hated it. I hate all this.
    CAROLINE: (sighs) I hate it, too.

    Bar

    [Stefan is writing in his diary when Alaric shows up]

    STEFAN: (voiceover) Dear Elena, where to begin?
    ALARIC: Looks like you could use that.
    STEFAN: Thanks. Just trying to figure out a fresh new way to deliver the same bad news.
    ALARIC: Well, I think your engagement would qualify as good news. I'm pretty sure I forgot to say congratulations earlier.
    STEFAN: Thanks. Though I'm not sure today's how I wanted to spend the first day of it. Not to mention my best man just tried to take out a good chunk of the wedding party.
    BARTENDER: Here you go.
    ALARIC: You can leave the bottle. Thanks. You know, I never had a brother. Uh, Damon was the closest thing. And I have to believe that if you have gotten him back before, there has got to be a way to do it again.
    STEFAN: I don't know. This is different. I've never seen him like this before. I really hoped that when we put Sybil down, it'd break whatever control she had over him. That he'd go back to being whatever Damon's version of normal is again. But somehow, some way, she still has ahold of him.

    [Damon is driving and Sybil can continue to control him. She is using her mind control ability to get inside his memories again and alter them, inserting herself into the his memory of his and Bonnie's time in the Salvatore Boarding House while in the Prison World]

    SYBIL: I didn't know you cooked.
    DAMON: Listen, you're gonna be here all the time, I am gonna need to get some more pancake batter.
    SYBIL: Well, I'm only here because you obviously want me here. I think you've seen the light, Damon. You know that true devotion to me is the only way to save you from that which you fear the most. But we still have a lot of work to do
    DAMON: Oh, yeah? What else is there to do?
    SYBIL: You still have too many attachments. Too many people willing to fight for you. They still believe you can be saved. We should work on that. What do they call it? Changing hearts and minds?

    Unknown Road

    [A driver pulls up to a body laying in the middle of the road. The drive is revealed to be Tyler Lockwood.]

    TYLER: Are you all right? Damon?
    DAMON: Lockwood.
    TYLER: Heard you haven't been yourself lately.
    DAMON: Oh, I am who I've always been. Just got to keep proving it.
    TYLER: Your fight's not with me, man.
    DAMON: Nice hardware. Armory standard issue?
    TYLER: You kill me, you can say good-bye to everything. No one forgives you for this.
    DAMON: I think we're way past that.
    TYLER: You have another option. Walk away. Fight whatever it is she wants you to do and get your life back. Walk away, Damon. Think about what you want at the end of it all. Think about who you want.
    DAMON: That's the problem. I don't want any of it anymore.
    TYLER: That's a lie.
    DAMON: Is it? I wouldn't even know.
    TYLER: Okay, then. Do it. If this is it, if this is the way I go out, then let me go out as the face that you can never erase from your brain. The face that reminds you, to the end of time, of the moment you gave up. The moment you threw away everything you've ever wanted... to be a siren's little bitch. Or maybe this'll be the thing that wakes you up. And then maybe both our lives will be worth something at the end of it all. So, go ahead. Do it. Tear me apart. [Tyler continues to provoke Damon. Sybil is continuing to influence Damon as well.] Do it! Damon?

    [Damon's face begins to change; he snarls, bearing his fangs]

    TYLER: Damon, wait!

    [Damon vamp speeds to Tyler and beings to feed, Tyler unable to resist him]
