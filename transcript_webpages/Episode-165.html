    PETER: Me running is the whole reason we're in this mess, Matt.
    MATT: Here's the difference, Dad, I want you to abandon me this time. Cos when I ring that bell all hell"s gonna break loose, literally.
    PETER: Leaving you and your sister was the biggest mistake of my life and I'll be damned if I'll do it again.
    MATT: No, Dad, you'll be damned if you don't.
    PETER: Maybe so, but if you wanna ring that bell, Matt, you're gonna have to go through me.
    MATT: Don't you get it, Dad, I will go through you, I'm compelled. I'm ringing that bell. Unless you kill me.
    PETER: What?! Matt. No. NO!
    MATT: You have to kill me. No matter what you do, I'll keep coming. I'll probably kill you in the process. Please, Dad, I'll die a hero. My life will mean something.
    PETER: I'm not laying a hand on you, son.
    MATT: You've been a crap father your whole life, why stop now?

    Damon's Subconscious (Graveyard)

    [Stefan chuckles]

    STEFAN: You're kidding me, right. Think of all the ways you have ruined my life for the last century, including the fact that I am working for Cade right now because of you and you seriously believe that you're the one who should be forgiving me.
    DAMON: You're right. I'm a monster. But, like I said, I forgive you.

    [Stefan punches Damon]

    STEFAN: I have nothing to apologise for.
    DAMON: Then why are you here, Stefan? Huh? Why are you always there to straighten me out when I go off the rails? C'mon man, you're not here for Cade. You're here because you blame yourself for turning me in the first place. Apparently I still do too. At least until today. I officially forgive you.
    STEFAN: Shut up!
    DAMON: I forgive you Stefan.

    [Stefan repeatedly punches Damon]

    STEFAN: Why aren't you healing?
    DAMON: Remember that vampire I was warning you about, running around in my head? Well, it's not me. It's you. It's always been you, Stefan. So, for the last time I forgive you for making me what I am. I absolve you of all the death and destruction I have caused as a result. And you wanna know why? Because you're my brother. And I love you and there's nothing you can do to ever change that.
    STEFAN: See, about that. You wanna forgive me for something bad, Damon? Well, how about this. I'm about to burn Mystic Falls to the ground.
    DAMON: What the hell you talking about?
    STEFAN: Hell is what I'm talking about. So before that beautiful brass bell rings, forgive me Damon. I dare you. And while you're at it, forgive me for coming here and trying to save you. Forgive me, for having the tiniest shred of guilt about leaving you to die with everyone else, for not seeing that the only way to get rid of you is to let you go up in flames with the rest of the damn town.

    Clock Tower

    [Bell rings]

    MATT: C'mon, Dad, do something.

    Salvatore Boarding House

    CAROLINE: Bonnie! Bonnie!
    SYBIL: Seline has the bell. You lied to me.
    STEFAN: Yeah, sorry. That was my bad. Oh, c'mon, don't look like that at me, Caroline. This is what you wanted, right? Til death do us part.

    [Damon wakes, grabs Stefan's heart.]

    DAMON: You're right, little brother. I can't forgive you for destroying our home town. Cos I ain't gonna let it happen.

    [Pulls Stefan's heart out.]

    Clock Tower

    MATT: C'mon, Dad, do something please. Dad, get up. Nine, ten. Dad, you're gonna have to kill me. Dad, c'mon. Eleven, please.
    PETER: I am so sorry, Matt.
    MATT: I forgive you.
    DAMON: Let's hope that one doesn't count.

    Clock Tower -- Outside

    DAMON: How you feeling, sport?
    MATT: Like I just had my head rammed into a large metal object.
    DAMON: Hashtag you're welcome. Your dad was looking kinda rough, too. I put the Hell Bell in the back of his truck and sent him on his way. You're not gonna do something stupid like chase after him, are you?
    MATT: Well, since Stefan compelled me to ring the bell by 9pm. I'd say we're in the clear. For the record, it's hard to thank you for something's that pretty much your fault.
    DAMON: Hey. Matt! I'm sorry about Vicki. I know you'll never forgive me for what I did. I should've told you this a long time ago.
    MATT: Let me guess, your emotions are back on, huh.
    DAMON: Yeah. It's gonna be a bumpy ride.
    MATT: Well, you did just save my father and pretty much the entire town so it counts for something in my book.
    DAMON: Does that mean we're good?
    MATT: Not by a long shot. But you keep on like the way you did tonight, I'm not ruling anything out.
    DAMON: Fair enough. Need a lift?
    MATT: Nah, gonna stop by the police department. Hear Mystic Falls needs a sheriff.
    DAMON: Always did like the sheriffs in this town.

    Salvatore Boarding House -- Basement

    STEFAN: Wow, you really can't take a hint.
    CAROLINE: Actually I can. So before you try tearing me down again I am fully aware that Damon means more to you than I do.
    STEFAN: Then why are you here?
    CAROLINE: Because I love you, Stefan. The other you, the good you. But this quarter of a man I'm staring at isn't you, not even remotely. So, if Damon is the answer to getting your humanity back, not me, fine. I will do whatever it takes. Then we will love each other forever or we won't. We'll make our relationship work or we won't. But either way, I'm getting you back.

    Salvatore Boarding House -- Outside

    DAMON: Hey.
    BONNIE: Hey, stranger.
    DAMON: Looks like I'm interrupting a reflective moment.
    BONNIE: It's fine. I'm reflecting on the good things for a change.
    DAMON: Wish I could say the same thing.
    BONNIE: What you did while you were under Sybil's control wasn't your fault, Damon. You didn't have a choice.
    DAMON: "Dear Bonnie. I'm a coward. I should be saying this to your face, not writing this letter but I know if I do, you'll talk me out of running away from all my problems. You're gonna make me face the future without Elena and you're gonna help make me the best man I can possibly be, same way she did. And I'm absolutely terrified of failing you both. So I'm leaving, because I'd rather let you down once than let you down for the rest of your life. And I hope it's the happiest life because you, Bonnie Bennett, are an amazing woman, a mediocre crossword puzzle player and my best friend. With great love and respect, Damon."
    BONNIE: Gotta admit, it's a hell of a letter.
    DAMON: I'm so sorry I left. It won't happen again.

    Diner

    SELINE: Why am I here, exactly? You are aware I just tried killing you.
    SYBIL: Can we stop, please, with the snarkiness and the attitude and the petty revenge schemes. It's exhausting.
    SELINE: I don't know. Can we?
    SYBIL: If Damon and Stefan Salvatore can make amends, then anything's possible.
    SELINE: Ok, I'll give it a shot. If you'll apologise for screwing me over with Cade.
    SYBIL: Me, apologise to you? You're the one who turned me into a cannibal and sold my soul into servitude and abandoned me for over a century.
    SELINE: You're right. And I'd do it all again. Because you've always been the weak one.
    SYBIL: Fine. Screw forgiveness.
    CADE: My beautiful children. You're still broken after all these years.
    SYBIL: Cade.
    CADE: In the flesh.
    SELINE: How are you here?
    CADE: The bell rang 11 times. One shy of hellfire upon this earth but enough for me to pay a visit.
    SELINE: We can do better, we'll work together.
    CADE: That won't be necessary. I'll handle it from here.

    [Both sirens burn in hellfire.]
